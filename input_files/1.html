    Photo Video Maker with Music

    Photo Video Maker with Music

    No 1 slideshow maker app of 2021. Share your amazing moments on Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, Snapchat, and Youtube.
    Turn your memories into movies with music. Slideshow Maker helps you to create SlideShow from your moments (Photos/Selfies).
    Share your amazing moment on Instagram, Facebook, twitter, Snapchat and YouTube.

    FEATURES:
    ========
    Transitions: Huge collection of transitions, themes & styles.
    Edit Photos: Edit your photos, add filters effects and reorder them.
    Add Music: Add music tracks, audio effects or voice to your slideshow.
    Own Music: Add your favourite music from your music collection
    Voice Overs: Record your own voice over the slideshow.
    Edit Audio: Trim, fade-in, fade-out & loop options for audio.
    Audio Sound: Volume control for audios. Video mute/unmute.
    Duration: Change the duration per photo.
    Share Slideshow: Share your amazing moment on Instagram, Facebook, twitter, YouTube, Snapchat,Vine or by E-mail.

    Premium Subscription Includes:
    * 100+ Attractive Slideshow Transitions.
    * 150+ Trending Musics.
    * 100+ Creative Filters.
    * Add Unlimited Photos.
    * Voice Over.
    * No Advertisement.
