    Description

    Pic Photo Slide Show Editor

    Pic Photo Slide Show Editor

    Use the Slideshow maker app, you can create beautiful musical slideshow video from photos and share them on your social media.
    We provide a large number of beautiful music slide show templates, it's easy to create a slideshow video with just a few simple steps.

    Simple Steps:
    1 - Pick some photos from your photo gallery
    2 - Choose the slideshow template you like
    3 - Add background music
    4 - Save slideshow video to album or share them on social media

    At the same time, we also provide frame adjustment and duration time adjustment functions, you can adjust it any way you want.
    Wish you enjoy using it
