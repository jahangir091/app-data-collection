    Description

    Slide show creator with music

    Slide show creator with music

    Create unforgettable slideshows in seconds with your photos & videos! Combine photo & video to make a movie with beautiful transitions, music, filters and effects and watch your media come alive. Amaze your friends and family and share your experiences on social media.

    Choose your own style from 17 fantastic themes including beach, chill, jolly, birthday and romantic!

    Features:
    - Pick photos & videos (up to 200 photos)
    - Set your own music or use the music from the included styles
    - Manage & reorder photo & video easily
    - Canvas sizes : Square, IG Story, IG Full, IG Landscape, Facebook & Pinterest
    - Fit or Fill canvas with blur background
    - Trim & Cut Video ( Basic & Advanced)
    - Flip, rotate photo or video
    - Set photo duration
    - Mute video audio
    - Landscape support for iPad

    Slide Show Premium Subscription Info:

    - You can subscribe for unlimited access to premium content, including: Unlocking all Features, Unlimited Photos / Videos and No Ads

    - You'll be able to use all features for the duration of your subscription

    - Payment will be charged to your iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase
